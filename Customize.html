How to Seamlessly Integrate Excel Spreadsheets with ProjectProdigy

Welcome to our tutorial on harnessing the power of ProjectProdigy to streamline your project management using Excel spreadsheets. Follow these steps to make your workflow more efficient:

Step 1: Adding Excel Spreadsheets to Your Project Template

If you use other Excel spreadsheets in your projects, you can easily add them to your project template:

  • Locate the Excel file you want to integrate, for example, an Excel pay application form.
  • Save it as a .xlsx file if it's not already in this format.
  • Open your project template in ProjectProdigy.
  • Add sheets or copy and paste data from the Excel file directly into the template.

Step 2: Extracting Formulas from Sheets

For example, if you have your own proposal or summary sheet, you can easily extract and use formulas from the RFPView sheet: (This requires Excel desktop)

  • Locate the RFPView sheet within your project template.
  • Unprotect the sheet and extract the formulas you need for your own sheet.
  • Simply paste these formulas into your custom sheet in the template.

Step 3: Unprotecting the Sheet

For seamless customization, you can unprotect the sheet on your desktop:

  • Access a sheet in your project template.
  • Remove any protection settings as needed.
  • This allows you to make changes and adjustments to suit your specific project requirements.
  • Remember to reapply the protection to the sheet then save the file.

Congratulations! You've successfully integrated Excel spreadsheets into your project management workflow using ProjectProdigy. Enjoy the benefits of a more organized and efficient project experience.
