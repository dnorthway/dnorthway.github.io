ProjectProdigy User Guide Help and Support

Install App

For installation instructions, watch the video here.

Installation: Right click and save the provided manifest as a .xml file to your computer. Upload the Project Template to your OneDrive and open it in Excel on the web. Select Add-ins, select Manage My Add-ins, select Upload My Add-in then upload the 'manifest.xml' file to install the app. Click the ProjectProdigy App Save Document button, the task pane will appear.

DASHBOARD

For dashboard guidance, watch the video here.

The Dashboard provides intuitive navigation and select filters for project information at a glance. Filter Requests for Information, Requests for Proposal, and Submittals by status or disposition.

CONTACTS

For contacts management, watch the video here.

Begin with Contacts. Begin by entering or pasting your Outlook project-specific contacts. These should include A/E, owner, subcontractors, and vendors. Access further help by clicking >HELP on the contacts sheet.

Capacity: Up to 2000 contacts.

Project Information

For managing project information, watch the video here.

Select Project, Owner, and A/E from contacts. Enter Superintendent's name, jobsite phone, and job number.

PURCHASE ORDER

For purchase order management, watch the video here.

Create Purchase Orders by filling in the shaded fields. Click the ProjectProdigy App Save Document button, and in the task pane, click the Save Purchase Order button. You can view and print POs and their respective logs.

Capacity: Up to 2000 purchase orders.

SUBCONTRACT CHANGE ORDER

For subcontract change order management, watch the video here.

Similar to Purchase Orders, enter or select information in shaded fields, save with the ProjectProdigy App, and view/print SCCOs and their logs.

Capacity: Up to 2000 Subcontract Change Orders.

REQUESTS FOR PROPOSAL

For requests for proposal management, watch the video here.

Requests for Proposal (RFP): Fill in the shaded fields, delete unused cells containing N\A, save with the app, and view/print RFPs, their logs, summaries, proposals, and budget effects.

To update the RFP, on the RFPView sheet click >RFP Status. Select the row of the RFP number and enter or select the information in the unshaded cells.

Capacity: Up to 2000 Request for Proposals.

REQUESTS FOR INFORMATION

For requests for information management, watch the video here.

Enter/select information, save, view, and print RFIs and their logs. You can also update RFIs by selecting the appropriate row on the RFI Status sheet and filling in unshaded cells.

Capacity: Up to 2000 Request for Information.

TRANSMITTALS/SUBMITTALS

For transmittals/submittals management, watch the video here.

Transmittals/Submittals: Select the specification section and provide relevant information. Delete any descriptions that are not being submitted at this time or add descriptions as needed, save, view, and print transmittals and their logs. Update by clicking >UpdateTransmittal on the TransmittalView sheet.

Capacity: Up to 4000 Transmittals.

Attachments

For managing attachments, watch the video here.

Utilize the cloud! Share your files and copy and paste the link in the Attachment cell on the form. Select the cell and paste the URL of your attachment in the formula bar. File must be stored on a cloud-based file-sharing site like OneDrive and shareable.

When the forms are printed with the Microsoft Print to PDF, the link is the attachment. This is very useful when the updated submittal log is published, the links on the PDF document are the submittals.

Tip: When attaching a submittal for review, give the A/E 'can edit' rights so it can be marked up in place. When the review is complete, the A/E can download it for record. After review, stop sharing and change the link to anyone with a link can view. This will make the submittal link available to anyone on the Submittal Log PDF document.
