Step 1: Open Outlook Desktop

Launch your Outlook Desktop application on your computer.

Step 2: Access People View

Click on the "People View" icon located in the lower-left corner of the Outlook window.

Step 3: Change View

In the "View" ribbon at the top of the screen, click "Change View" and select "Manage Views."

Select "List" and click "Modify..."

Click "Columns..."

Select "Address Fields" and add the following fields in order:

  • Business Address Street
  • Business Address City
  • Business Address State
  • Business Address Postal Code

Make sure the fields are in the specified order.

  • Icon
  • Attachment
  • Flag Status
  • Full Name
  • Job Title
  • Company
  • File As
  • Country/Region
  • Department
  • Business Phone
  • Business Fax
  • Business Address Street
  • Business Address City
  • Business Address State
  • Business Address Postal Code
  • Home Phone
  • Mobile Phone
  • Email
  • Categories
  • Assistance Phone

Show the columns in the order shown. Select Apply, select your contacts then press "Ctrl+C".

Step 16: Paste in ProjectManager

Switch to your ProjectProdigy app. In the "Contacts" section, locate the cell labeled "Paste Outlook 365 Contacts Here."

Step 17: Paste Contacts

Click on the cell and press "Ctrl + V" to paste the copied Outlook contacts into ProjectProdigy.

Step 18: Fill in Missing Information

Review the pasted contacts and fill in any missing information, such as project-specific details or additional contact information.

Congratulations! You've successfully pasted your Outlook contacts into ProjectProdigy, enhancing your project management process.
